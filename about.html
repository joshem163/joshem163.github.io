Academic Journey

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Mathematics at UT Dallas, specializing in Numerical Modeling and Topological Machine Learning on graphs, with a focus on power grid networks. . My research aims to improve graph representation learning through topological encodings. In my current research, I focus on integrating topological data analysis (TDA) with graph neural networks (GNNs) to address limitations in existing graph representation learning. I developed two novel models, Wise-GNN and ClassContrast, which address fundamental challenges faced by Graph Neural Networks (GNNs).
